Research work

Small sized sediments stay suspended on surface water and called susupended sediment. Suspended sediments absorb and scatter light in a different wavelength than that of water. Hence using the wavelengths we can quantify the concentration of suspended sediments from satellite images. 

I have used MODIS ocean color images, and AD-QAA technique to find out the amount of scattering of suspended sediment.

I have used ANN technique to develop an algorithm for scattering and suspended sediment concentration. Then I have studied 20 years of suspended sediment using the developed algorithm
